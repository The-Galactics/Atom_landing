# Core Idea [Atom].

Currently, the mobile device ecosystem lacks **simplicity** when performing **tasks** or **processes** intended for users. This causes situations where, without prior **knowledge**, there is greater difficulty in adapting to multiple **customization layers** such as HyperOS, OneUI, or Nothing OS, which increase the system's learning curve. The lack of a unified interface creates accessibility barriers for people with limited technological knowledge.

## What is Atom?

**Atom** is a mobile device platform that enables interaction with an **AI** (Artificial Intelligence) **assistant** that **adapts** to the user and their needs. It seeks to be a **unique assistant** in its style, having the capacity to **learn** and **coordinate** to facilitate tasks and processes on **mobile devices**. Furthermore, it aims to integrate **assistive technology** to help individuals with greater **difficulties** in the **technological field** or any **disability** that hinders device interaction, such as motor or visual impairments. **Atom** aims to break this pattern, seeking **simplicity** without requiring any **knowledge** beyond downloading a mobile **app**.

## What makes Atom different from other assistants?

The goal is to create and innovate. **Atom** is a generative AI assistant that works in **real-time** to fulfill user requests, but it doesn't just limit itself to **listening** and responding; it goes further—it **creates**, **plans**, and **acts**. If you don't know how to send an email, **Atom** **learns** it by **searching** and **planning** the action to be performed. Utilizing an **Orchestrator AI** and a **Developer AI**, it **creates** and **saves** the process to do it better and faster next time.

It can interpret **natural language** provided via **voice commands** or user **text**, allowing for **practicality** and **simplicity**. However, **Atom** does not focus on just one niche; **Atom** seeks to facilitate your **tasks** and be your companion beyond mere **instructions** and **results**. **Atom** can help you create **files**, interact directly with your **device**, and **control** it for you (always within a secure and private environment where the user has the final word over the assistant's actions), enabling **automation** at the **operating system** level through **voice** or **written** commands.
